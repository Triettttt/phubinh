PEARL - TIMELESS ELEGANCE

Pearl - Timeless Elegance originates from the mid-19th century, when French colonists invaded southern Vietnam and recognized the endless potential of the tropical seas. They established the Pearl Jewelry Workshop to exploit and export precious pearls. With support from the French government, the workshop continued to grow, merging with and acquiring many other jewelry workshops, eventually forming the Pearl - Timeless Elegance jewelry company.

Team members: Trương Thiên Bảo - Nguyễn Phú Bình - Nguyễn Thị Trà My - Lý Gia Kiệt
Supervising Lecturer: Thầy Châu Đức Vũ


TLINH'S SÁNG CONCERT

Light Label is an entertainment company founded in 2024 with the goal of becoming a pioneering force in the music industry. The company aims to deliver many products tailored for customers passionate about distinctive and unique music. Light Label continuously explores and nurtures new talents, builds the Vietnamese music market, and expands its influence to the international market.
Currently, Light Label manages and develops promising artists, most notably Nguyễn Thảo Linh (Tlinh). A talented young artist in the R&B/Soul and Vietnamese Hip Hop genres, she is one of the co-founders of Light Label. The company's unique music is targeted towards Gen Z audiences.


Team members: Trương Thiên Bảo - Nguyễn Phú Bình - Nguyễn Thị Trà My
Supervising Lecturer: Ms. Đặng Mai Hương Quề

JUJUTSU KAISEN CHARACTER BOOK

Overviewing about the world of Jujutsu Kaisen, the character book showcases the series of unforgettable heroes, villains, and cursed spirits. Featuring detailed profiles, backstories, and stunning artwork, this guide uncovers the powers and personalities that shape the story’s intense battles and relationships. Whether you're a devoted fan or a newcomer, this book offers a deeper look into the characters that define the supernatural world of Jujutsu Kaisen.
